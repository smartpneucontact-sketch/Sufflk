# Section 04 22 00 — Concrete Unit Masonry

**Project:** Allerton Tower (synthetic demo)
**Issued:** 2026-01-18  |  **Rev:** 01

## Part 1 — General

### 1.01 Summary
Includes CMU partitions, stair shafts, and elevator shaft walls. Coordinate with Sections 04 05 13 (Mortar and Masonry Grout) and 04 05 23 (Masonry Accessories).

### 1.03 Environmental Requirements
Do not lay masonry units when air temperature is below 40°F or above 100°F without protection conforming to ACI 530.1 Article 1.8.

## Part 2 — Products

### 2.01 Units
ASTM C90 normal-weight units. Compressive strength f'm = 2,000 psi minimum, verified by unit-strength method or prism testing per CM's election.

### 2.02 Reinforcing in Grouted Cells
Bars centered in cell width unless noted. Lap splices Class B per ACI 318. Vertical bars at jamb conditions shall be #5 minimum, doweled to footing.

## Part 3 — Execution

### 3.04 Tolerances
- Bed joint thickness: 3/8 in. ± 1/8 in.
- Head joint thickness: 3/8 in. ± 1/8 in.
- Variation from plumb in any story or 10 ft: 1/4 in.
- Variation from level top of bond beam: 1/4 in. in any bay or 10 ft.

### 3.06 Grouting
High-lift grouting permitted up to 12 ft 8 in. lifts when cleanouts are provided at base of grout pour. Low-lift grouting limited to 5 ft 4 in. lifts without cleanouts. Consolidate grout with mechanical vibrator immediately after placement; reconsolidate within 30 minutes.

### 3.08 Anchorage to Structure
Top-of-wall anchorage at slab soffit shall accommodate vertical deflection per S-101. Provide approved compressible filler at slab interface.
