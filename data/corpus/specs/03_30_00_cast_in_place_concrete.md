# Section 03 30 00 — Cast-in-Place Concrete

**Project:** Allerton Tower (synthetic demo) — 32-story mixed-use, Boston, MA
**Issued:** 2026-01-15  |  **Rev:** 03

## Part 1 — General

### 1.01 Summary
This Section includes cast-in-place concrete for foundations, walls, columns, slabs-on-grade, and elevated slabs. Coordinate with Sections 03 20 00 (Reinforcement) and 03 11 00 (Formwork).

### 1.02 Submittals
Submit mix designs, batch tickets, and curing-method certificates to the Construction Manager not less than 14 calendar days prior to first placement. Mix designs older than 12 months require re-validation.

## Part 2 — Products

### 2.01 Concrete Mix Designs
- Foundation walls: 5,000 psi @ 28 days, w/c ratio ≤ 0.45, max aggregate 3/4 in.
- Interior columns (L1–L8): 6,000 psi @ 56 days, low-shrinkage admixture required.
- Interior columns (L9–L32): 8,000 psi @ 56 days, low-shrinkage admixture required.
- Slabs-on-grade: 4,500 psi @ 28 days, fiber reinforcement permitted in lieu of WWF where indicated.
- Elevated slabs: 5,000 psi @ 28 days, max slump 5 in. before high-range water reducer.

### 2.02 Reinforcing
ASTM A615 Grade 60 deformed bars. Epoxy-coated (ASTM A775) at exterior exposure surfaces and slabs-on-grade in contact with vapor barrier penetrations.

## Part 3 — Execution

### 3.01 Placement
No placement during ambient temperatures below 40°F without an approved cold-weather plan per ACI 306. No placement during ambient temperatures above 90°F without an approved hot-weather plan per ACI 305.

### 3.04 Reinforcing Cover
Minimum concrete cover over outermost reinforcing:
- Cast against and permanently exposed to earth: 3 in.
- Exposed to earth or weather (#6 bars and larger): 2 in.
- Exposed to earth or weather (#5 bars and smaller): 1-1/2 in.
- **Interior columns, beams, and walls: 1-1/2 in.**
- Interior slabs and joists (#11 and smaller): 3/4 in.
- Pile caps and footings cast against earth: 3 in.

Deviations require written approval from the Structural Engineer of Record (EOR) prior to placement.

### 3.06 Curing
Wet cure for not less than 7 days for normal-weight concrete and 14 days for high-strength (≥ 6,000 psi) concrete. Curing compounds permitted only on surfaces not receiving topping or bonded finishes.

### 3.09 Tolerances
Slab flatness FF35 / levelness FL25 in occupied areas. Column verticality tolerance: 1/4 in. in 10 ft, not exceeding 1 in. total.
