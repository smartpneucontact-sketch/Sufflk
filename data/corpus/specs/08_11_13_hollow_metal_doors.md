# Section 08 11 13 — Hollow Metal Doors and Frames

**Project:** Allerton Tower (synthetic demo)
**Issued:** 2026-01-25  |  **Rev:** 01

## Part 1 — General

### 1.01 Summary
Includes hollow metal doors, frames, and transom panels at stair shafts, mechanical rooms, electrical rooms, and rated egress corridors. Refer to A-601 door schedule and A-651 hardware sets.

## Part 2 — Products

### 2.01 Doors
- Interior, non-rated: SDI A60-2000, Level 2, 16 ga. face sheets.
- Interior, fire-rated: SDI A250.4, Level 3, 14 ga. face sheets, labeled per door schedule.
- Stair pressurization doors: Level 4, 14 ga. with reinforced edge for hardware specified in 08 71 00.

### 2.02 Frames
Welded, 16 ga. minimum at non-rated; 14 ga. at rated. Frames at exterior or wet locations shall be galvanized per ASTM A653 G60.

## Part 3 — Execution

### 3.03 Installation
Anchor frames per manufacturer's anchorage schedule; minimum 3 anchors per jamb at openings 7 ft 0 in. or less, 4 anchors per jamb above 7 ft 0 in. Frames at CMU walls shall be filled solid with grout in cells adjacent to the frame.

### 3.05 Tolerances and Clearances
Door undercut at hard-surface flooring: 3/4 in. maximum. At carpet, undercut shall accommodate carpet plus pad without binding. Clearance between door and frame stop: 1/8 in. ± 1/16 in.
