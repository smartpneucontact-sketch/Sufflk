# Section 05 12 00 — Structural Steel Framing

**Project:** Allerton Tower (synthetic demo)
**Issued:** 2026-01-22  |  **Rev:** 02

## Part 1 — General

### 1.01 Summary
Includes structural steel columns, beams, girders, and connection plates for podium levels L1–L6 and rooftop mechanical screen. Refer to S-301 through S-318.

### 1.02 Quality Assurance
Fabricator shall be AISC-certified, Category Bu (Conventional Steel Building Structures). Erector shall be AISC-certified, Category CSE (Certified Steel Erector).

## Part 2 — Products

### 2.01 Materials
- Wide-flange shapes: ASTM A992 Grade 50.
- HSS sections: ASTM A500 Grade C.
- Anchor rods: ASTM F1554 Grade 55 with weldable supplement.
- High-strength bolts: ASTM F3125 Grade A325 unless A490 is shown.

### 2.03 Connections
Simple-shear, fully-restrained moment, and partially-restrained connections shall be designed by Fabricator's licensed engineer in the project state. Connection design submittals due 21 days prior to fabrication.

## Part 3 — Execution

### 3.02 Erection Tolerances
Per AISC Code of Standard Practice section 7.13. Column out-of-plumb tolerance shall not exceed 1:500 of the column height, or 1 in., whichever is smaller.

### 3.05 Field Welding
Field welds shall be performed by AWS D1.1-certified welders. Welder qualifications shall be on file with the CM prior to first weld. CJP welds at moment connections require 100% UT inspection.

### 3.07 Bolting
Pretensioned and slip-critical connections require Skidmore-Wilhelm or equivalent calibration daily, and turn-of-nut or DTI verification. All bolts in moment frames are slip-critical.

### 3.09 Cold Weather Welding
No field welding at ambient temperatures below 32°F without preheat per AWS D1.1 Table 5.8. Preheat shall be applied to minimum 3 in. each side of joint and verified by temperature crayon or contact pyrometer.
