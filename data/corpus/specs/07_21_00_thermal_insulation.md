# Section 07 21 00 — Thermal Insulation

**Project:** Allerton Tower (synthetic demo)
**Issued:** 2026-01-20  |  **Rev:** 01

## Part 1 — General

### 1.01 Summary
Includes board insulation at exterior walls, cavity insulation at metal-framed partitions adjacent to demising lines, and roof insulation under PVC membrane (refer to 07 54 19).

### 1.02 Performance Requirements
Continuous insulation R-value at exterior wall assemblies: R-20 minimum per IECC 2021 Climate Zone 5A. Building thermal envelope shall achieve overall U-factor not greater than 0.045 per energy model.

## Part 2 — Products

### 2.01 Exterior Wall Continuous Insulation
Mineral fiber board (ASTM C612 Type IVB), nominal density 8 pcf, R-4.3 per inch. Thickness as indicated, not less than 4 in. continuous.

### 2.02 Cavity Insulation
Mineral wool batts (ASTM C665 Type I, unfaced), R-15 minimum at 2x4 framing, R-21 minimum at 2x6 framing.

## Part 3 — Execution

### 3.03 Installation at Exterior Walls
Insulation boards shall be tight-fitted; joints staggered both horizontally and vertically. Gaps wider than 1/8 in. shall be filled with closed-cell spray foam or backer rod and approved sealant. Mechanically fasten per manufacturer's instructions with fasteners through clip locations; no penetrations through the air barrier outside clip footprints.

### 3.05 Coordination with Air and Water-Resistive Barriers
Insulation shall be installed only after AWB inspection sign-off per Section 07 27 00. Any AWB damage during insulation install shall be repaired and re-inspected before cladding is placed.
