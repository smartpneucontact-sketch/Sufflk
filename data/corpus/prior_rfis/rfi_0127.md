**RFI-0127**  |  Project: Allerton Tower  |  Submitted: 2026-03-04  |  Closed: 2026-03-07
**Discipline:** Masonry  |  **Trade:** Masonry Sub
**References:** Spec 04 22 00 §3.06

**Question:**
We propose 14 ft high-lift grouting at stair shaft SS-2 east jamb to keep pace with structural steel above. Spec limits high-lift to 12 ft 8 in. Requesting deviation given access constraints once steel is erected.

**Response (Structural EOR):**
Deviation denied. High-lift limit is set by ACI 530.1 to assure grout consolidation. Resequence with steel erector to place grout within 12 ft 8 in. lifts before steel above is erected. Coordinate with CM scheduler.

**Resolution:** Resequenced. Mason completed grout 2026-03-06; steel erection resumed 2026-03-07.
**Schedule impact:** none (absorbed in float)  |  **Cost impact:** none  |  **Escalated to EOR:** yes
