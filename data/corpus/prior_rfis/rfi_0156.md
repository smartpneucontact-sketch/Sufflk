**RFI-0156**  |  Project: Allerton Tower  |  Submitted: 2026-03-28  |  Closed: 2026-04-04
**Discipline:** Doors and Frames  |  **Trade:** GC self-perform / Doors Sub
**References:** Spec 08 11 13 §3.03

**Question:**
At CMU walls flanking stair shafts SS-1 and SS-3, current jamb anchor configuration provides only 2 anchors per jamb at door openings 7'-0" tall. Spec requires 3 minimum. Existing CMU is grouted solid at jamb cells per drawing. Can we proceed without adding the third anchor?

**Response (CM + EOR):**
No. The 3-anchor minimum per 08 11 13 §3.03 is independent of grout condition. Provide third anchor via epoxy adhesive anchor (Hilti HIT-HY 200 or equal) at mid-jamb height. Submit installation procedure for approval prior to installation. Pull-test minimum 10% of installed anchors.

**Resolution:** Adhesive anchors installed and pull-tested 4/3. All passed.
**Schedule impact:** 2 days  |  **Cost impact:** ~$2,400 (Doors Sub)  |  **Escalated to EOR:** yes
